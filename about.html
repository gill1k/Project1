India: Land of Diversity

India is the fifth largest and the second populated country in the world. India is one of the few countries which can boast of an ancient, deep-rooted, and diverse culture. There is an endless diversity in India starting from its physical features to geologic structure, fauna and flora, demographic structure, races, languages, and religions.India is sometimes described as "the Mini World", the "epitome of the world" and an "ethnological museum".Diversity in India is unique.
