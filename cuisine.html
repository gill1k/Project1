Cuisine

Indian has so many different cuisines. Two main Indian cuisines are North Indian and South Indian cuisine. North Indian cuisine has a wide variety of curries e.g. palak paneer, paneer kofta, khadai paneer, butter chicken, chicken tikka masala etc. All these curries are mainly served with chapati or naan. South Indian cuisine mainly include Dosa, Uttapam, Idli, Upma etc.. South Indian cuisine is mainly served with chutney and sambhar. There are other famous Indian cuisines too other than these two, Bengali cuisine is known for its fish curries and mouth watering sweets, Maharashtra cuisine is known for Vada Pao and Dabeli. In Rajasthani cuisine Dal-Bati-Churma is the most famous.Gujarati cuisine and East Indian cuisine is quiet famous too.
